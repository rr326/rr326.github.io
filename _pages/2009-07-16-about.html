Hi.  My name is Ross.

I'm a startup guy, I guess. By rough counting, I think I've worked for about 25 companies over the years. Many have been short consulting engagements, but several have been long term stints. I've been a software engineer, a product manager, a VP of Marketing, a head of Sales, a founder and CEO, and a GetStuffDone consultant.  While every company is different, after awhile, patterns emerge.

In this incarnation of my blog, I'm going to share some of the patterns I've learned. I will not pretend that the insights I share will be original, brilliant, or perfectly articulated. But they'll be mine, and hopefully they will be useful to some. Or at least enjoyable to me to write.

So here goes.
